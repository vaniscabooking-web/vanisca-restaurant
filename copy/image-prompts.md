# Vanisca — Image Generation Prompts (approved list only)

Rules: generate **only** the assets listed in `asset-plan.md`, one approval per
group, no extra variations. Every prompt inherits the **base style block** and
the **restrictions block**. These are artistic motion-design visuals for the
website experience (owner policy in `copy/README.md`) — creative direction is
free and premium culinary/interior artistry is encouraged; they are not menu
photography, which stays real.

## Base style block (append to every prompt)

> Ultra-cinematic luxury restaurant photography style. Dark charcoal base
> (#0d0b09), warm candlelit gold key light (#c8a45c, highlights toward #e6cd93),
> deep soft shadows, gentle atmospheric haze, shallow depth of field, editorial
> stillness, Mediterranean fine-dining mood. Muted olive, sand and copper
> support tones. Calm negative space for website text overlays.
> Apple-level premium composition. Michelin Guide editorial photography
> quality. Luxury campaign quality. Architectural visualization precision.
> Ultra-premium cinematic storytelling.

## Restrictions block (append to every prompt)

> No text. No logos. No watermarks. No people or faces. No bright studio
> lighting. No neon or cool blue grading. No cluttered tables.
